# VirtualBank

Welcome to our VirtualBank software! The purpose of this software is to teach children how to develop better saving habits. In this software, children can earn money by completing tasks assigned by their parents and keep it in their virtual bank accounts. They can set saving goals for each account to motivate themselves to save money to buy their favorite toys. At the same time, parents can easily monitor their children's account transactions and other information to supervise them. Below is a simple guide on how to operate the software:
### System Requirements:

- JDK Version: OpenJDK 21
- Operating System Recommendation: MacOS

### Possible Issues:

- This software is developed on MacOS, so it is recommended to run the software on MacOS for optimal performance.
- If the background images fail to load, it may be due to compatibility issues with the operating system.
### 0. Running the Program:
   To start using the software, we need to run the program located at "src/login/Main.java": ![Execute Program](./ScreenShot/execute.png)

### 1. Registration and Login:
   On the login page, if you haven't registered yet, you can click the "Sign up" button to go to the registration page: ![Login Page](./ScreenShot/login.png)
   On the registration page, you can choose the account type, including parent and kid, and enter your personal information such as email to register: ![Registration Page](./ScreenShot/signup.png)

### 2. For Kids:

#### Functionality Provided for Kids:
We have prepared various features for child users, including:
- Creating an account
- Viewing account information
- Depositing and withdrawing money
- Transferring money
- Checking transaction details
- Completing tasks to earn money
- Setting saving goals
- Personalizing avatar and theme

You can switch between different pages by clicking on various buttons on the top navigation bar. Let's start by creating an account!

#### 2.1 Creating an Account:
After logging in, click the button to create a new account: ![Kid Function](./ScreenShot/kidfunction.png)
Choose the account type and set the name and password for the account: ![Create Account](./ScreenShot/createaccount.png)

#### 2.2 Selecting Accounts and Checking Account Information:
Use the arrows on the sides of the page to switch between different accounts. Click the "Detail" button to view account details, including deposits, transactions, and perform deposit/withdrawal and transfer operations: ![Check Detail](./ScreenShot/checkdetail.png) ![Detail Page](./ScreenShot/detailpage.png)
Here, you can see detailed information for each account: ![Check Information](./ScreenShot/checkinformation.png)

#### 2.3 Earning Money by Completing Tasks:
Click the "Task" button on the sidebar to access the task page. Here, you can view tasks assigned by parents. After completing a task, simply click it to notify parents. Once approved, you'll receive the corresponding reward: ![Submit Task](./ScreenShot/submittask.png)

#### 2.4 Setting Saving Goals:
Click the "Goal" button on the sidebar to access the savings goal page. Here, you can view, set, and delete saving goals. Click the button at the bottom of the page to create a new saving goal: ![Create Saving Goal](./ScreenShot/createsavinggoal.png)

#### 2.5 Changing Avatar and Theme:
Click the "Profile" button on the sidebar to access the personal information page. Here, you can view various personal information and change your avatar by clicking on it. You can also click the "Change Theme" button to switch to a theme you like: ![Theme](./ScreenShot/theme.png)

### 3. For Parents:

#### Functionality Provided for Parents:
We have prepared various features for parent users, including:
- Binding child accounts
- Viewing child accounts
- Setting tasks
- Receiving notifications for child task submissions
- Personalizing avatar and theme

After logging in, parents can switch between different pages by clicking on various buttons on the top navigation bar. ![Function](./ScreenShot/function.png)

#### 3.1 Viewing Child Account Information:
At the bottom of the home page, click the "Check my kid's account" button to view a list of each child's account information. Click on the account you want to view to see its details and transaction records: ![Check Kid Account](./ScreenShot/checkkidaccount.png)

#### 3.2 Setting Tasks for Children:
Click the "Task" button on the sidebar to access the task setting page. Here, you can assign, manage, and modify tasks for your children. Click the "Create a Task" button to create a task by entering its description and the corresponding reward amount. After setting up the task, click the "Deliver a task" button to assign it to the child: ![Parent Task](./ScreenShot/parenttask.png) ![Create Task](./ScreenShot/createtask.png) ![Deliver Task](./ScreenShot/delivertask.png)

#### 3.3 Viewing Notifications:
Click the "Notification" button on the sidebar to access the notification page. Here, you'll receive notifications when children complete and submit tasks. As a parent, you can accept or reject their submission. If approved, the corresponding reward will be deposited into their account: ![Notification](./ScreenShot/notification.png) ![Notification 2](./ScreenShot/notification2.png)

#### 3.4 Binding Children, Changing Avatar and Theme:
Click the "Profile" button on the sidebar to access the personal information page. Here, you can view various personal information. Click "Add my kid" to bind your child's account. This allows you to view their account information and assign tasks. You can also change your avatar by clicking on it or switch to a theme you like by clicking the "Change Theme" button: ![Profile](./ScreenShot/profile.png)
